A Quick Start Guide to Online Membership Application

Welcome to the quick start guide. This document outlines the comprehensive steps required to complete your online membership application successfully.

▶
Watch Tutorial

1. How to Apply

The application process is designed to be seamless. You will need to prepare the following items before starting:

  • Personal Information: Full name, address, and contact details.
  • Documents Required: Scanned copies of valid ID and proof of billing.
  • Agreement: Review and accept the Terms of Service.
Pro Tip: Ensure your documents are in PDF or JPG format and do not exceed 5MB each to avoid upload errors.

2. Monitoring Your Application

Once submitted, you will receive a Tracking Code via email. Use this code to check your status on the dashboard.

3. Application Process Flow

Here is what happens after you click submit:

  1. System assigns your application to a review officer.
  2. Officer verifies documents (Processing).
  3. Notification is sent regarding Approval or Disapproval.
